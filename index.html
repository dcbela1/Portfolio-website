About Me

Hi, I’m Michael — a motivated learner and creative problem-solver with a passion for web development, design, and building digital projects from the ground up. I enjoy experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to turn ideas into functional and visually appealing websites.

Outside of coding, I’ve worked on projects involving property listing sites, email marketing campaigns, and crypto tracking tools. These experiences have helped me strengthen my skills in communication, organization, and creative design, while also showing me the importance of making technology both useful and easy to understand.

My goal is to keep learning, refining my craft, and eventually create tools, sites, and resources that make an impact on people’s everyday lives. This portfolio is where I share my progress as I grow and push myself to the next level.
